### Quantitative Economics (Summer 2024)  
**Professor Thomas Sargent**, **Professor John Stachurski**, **Professor Matt McKay**

- Graduate-level course in quantitative and computational economics.
- Topics included dynamic programming, stochastic processes, and numerical methods.
- Assisted students with computational implementation and graded assignments.

### Computational and Algorithmic Methods (R & Python) (Spring 2024)  
**Professor Gábor Nyéki (Princeton University)**, **Professor Zimé Songbian**

- Statistical programming and algorithmic methods using **R** and **Python**.
- Topics included data manipulation, simulation, optimization, and introductory machine learning.
- Led lab sessions and provided feedback on coding assignments.

### Econometrics (2024)  
**Professor Tamoghna Hader**

- Undergraduate econometrics.
- Topics included OLS, hypothesis testing, and applied regression analysis.
- Supported empirical projects using **R**, **Stata**, and **Python**.
